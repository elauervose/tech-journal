The Belmont Menagerie

Come stay in a cozy two bedroom condo on Belmont Street in SE Portland with two humans and five friendly, fuzzy friends!

This is a home for animal lovers who don't mind sharing space with four legged house mates and everything that comes with that.

That being said, we make every effort to keep the space clean and comfortable for everyone. We clean litter boxes and pet areas every day and have air filters in every room of the house. No pets are allowed in guest room to keep dander and pet hair to an absolute minimum.

There is absolutely no expectation that you as a guest interact with animals unless you want to - in which case, there are treats and toys to facilitate friend making!

Our place is located right in the heart of the Buckman-SunnySide neighborhood (inner Southeast Portland) with cafes, restaurants, coffee shops, and boutiques just a few blocks away. The #15 bus stops 1.5 blocks from our house every 15 minutes and takes you straight downtown where you can connect with just about any bus to take you anywhere in Portland via the astoundingly fantastic transit system.

We even have a new food cart pod starting up a block away where you can currently get some pretty great food!

We really love Portland and love sharing our favorite places with visitors! Just name your fancy and we can point you to any number of the weird, wonderful, and absolutely beautiful cornucopia of Portland sites to see!
