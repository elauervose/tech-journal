About Me

I wasn't always a "techie" and I don't always qualify even now ...

I began my adult life working in nonprofits and became interested in the ways technology could benefit organizations. I quickly realized there is still a lot of learning to do and jumped in head first. .... literally.

This blog is a repository of things I've learned that I want to be able to share with you. My goal in sharing this is to help people solving social problems learn enough tech to comfortably engage in decision making and more effectively implement technological solutions.

You can read more about me on my secret "about-me" page, or you can check out my portfolio if you really want to read a lot more about me.
